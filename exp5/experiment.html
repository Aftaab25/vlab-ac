Perform Experiment

Procedure

  1. Connect the different components of the given Instrumentation Amplifier Circuit.
  2. After connecting the different components, check whether the connections are correct or not.
  3. If the connections are correct, proceed to the slider and vary the value of temperature to change the value of resistance in the thermistor.
  4. After changing the values, click on the "add" button. This will add the value to the Observation Table.
  5. Repeat the experiment 5 times to fill the table completely.
